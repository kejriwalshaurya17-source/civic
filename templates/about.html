About This Project

This project was developed for Hackathon 2025 to provide an easy-to-use civic engagement platform.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between citizens and ward members by enabling transparent communication and faster problem resolution.

Key Features

  • Report civic issues in real-time.
  • Ward member login & registration system.
  • Centralized contact and issue tracking.
  • Simple, user-friendly interface.
